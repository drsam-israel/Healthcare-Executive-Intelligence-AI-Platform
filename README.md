# 📊 Key Quantifiable Findings

## Operational Intelligence

- Total admissions analyzed: ~5,000
- Average LOS: ~5 days
- ED utilization: ~45%
- Elevated operational congestion observed in ED workflows

## Quality & Risk Analytics

- Overall readmission rate: ~18%
- Overall mortality rate: ~5–6%
- Highest readmission burden observed in Emergency and Pediatrics
- Highest mortality burden observed in Surgery and Obstetrics

## Financial Intelligence

- Average estimated cost per admission: ~$8,500
- ICU and Emergency demonstrated highest cumulative operational cost burden

## Explainable AI Findings

SHAP identified:
- LOS,
- estimated cost,
- diagnosis,
- department,
- and payer type

as the strongest operational drivers of readmission risk prediction.
