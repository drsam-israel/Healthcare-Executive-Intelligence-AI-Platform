# Healthcare Executive Intelligence & Predictive AI Platform

An executive-grade healthcare analytics and AI platform for hospital operations intelligence, readmission risk prediction, explainable AI, and admissions forecasting.

## Project Overview

This project simulates a hospital executive intelligence system designed for healthcare leaders, operations teams, and digital health stakeholders.

The platform provides:

- Executive KPI monitoring
- Hospital operations intelligence
- Quality and risk analytics
- Financial and payer analytics
- AI-powered readmission prediction
- SHAP explainability
- Admissions forecasting
- Executive AI-generated summaries

## Tech Stack

- Python
- Pandas
- Streamlit
- Plotly
- Scikit-learn
- XGBoost
- SHAP
- Matplotlib

## Key Features

### Executive Dashboard
Tracks total admissions, average length of stay, readmission rate, mortality rate, ED admission rate, and average cost.

### Operations Intelligence
Analyzes admissions volume, departmental utilization, and length-of-stay patterns.

### Quality & Risk Analytics
Evaluates readmission and mortality patterns across departments and diagnoses.

### Financial Analytics
Analyzes payer mix, average cost, and departmental cost burden.

### AI Readmission Predictor
Uses an XGBoost model to predict 30-day hospital readmission risk.

### Explainable AI
Uses SHAP to explain global and patient-level drivers of readmission risk.

### Operational Forecasting
Forecasts future hospital admissions to support capacity planning and resource allocation.
